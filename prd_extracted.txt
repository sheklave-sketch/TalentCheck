1. Introduction
Problem
Hiring in Ethiopia is:
CV-driven and referral-heavy
Highly biased (school, background, connections)
Time-consuming for HR teams
Poor at predicting real job performance
Difficult to scale for high-volume roles
There is no strong local, standardized pre-employment testing platform adapted for:
Ethiopian education system
Local language needs
Ethiopian labor market realities
Solution
A SaaS-based pre-employment testing platform that enables employers to:
Screen candidates objectively
Reduce bias
Test job-relevant skills
Conduct remote proctored assessments
Rank candidates automatically
2. Vision
To become Ethiopia’s most trusted hiring assessment platform that helps organizations hire based on skills, not CVs.
